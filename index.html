Allie Griffin is a journalist and designer.

She currently works as a metro reporter for the New York Post. She previously wrote for her hometown paper the Staten Island Advance. She also writes about the ethical fashion movement and abuses faced by garment factory workers for the nonprofit Remake . Allie previously served as the Senior News Editor of The New School Free Press, The New School's independent student-run newspaper.

Allie graduted from The New School in May 2018 with degrees in Fashion Design and Journalism + Design.
