About

Allie is both a journalist and designer.

She is finishing her fifth year at The New School, where she will receieve a BA in Journalism + Design and a BFA in Fashion Design from Parsons School of Design. She completed her fashion design thesis, a series of textile portraits and stories of garment factory workers living and working in Cambodia, last year. In her studies, she has pursued interests in graphic design, typography, profile writing and investigative reporting.

A native New Yorker, Allie now calls Brooklyn home, where she resides with an orange cat and a pint of coffee ice cream hidden in her freezer at all times.
