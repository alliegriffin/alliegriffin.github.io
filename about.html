About

Allie currently works as a metro reporter for the New York Post. Before joining the Post, Allie reported and wrote for her hometown paper the Staten Island Advance. She also writes about the ethical fashion movement and garment factory workers for the nonprofit Remake.

Allie graduted from The New School in May 2018 with degrees in Journalism + Design and Fashion Design. During her studies, she was the senior news editor of the student paper and she pursued interests in graphic design, typography, web design, coding, data visualization, videography, podcasting and investigative reporting.

A native New Yorker, Allie now calls Brooklyn home, where she resides with an orange cat and a pint of coffee ice cream hidden in her freezer at all times.
